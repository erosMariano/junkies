08Can I choose the art of my Surf Junkie NFT?

Buying a surf junkie NFT at the initial sale won't be like choosing a product from a shelf. Here's how it works: once you confirm your purchase, the smart contract runs a code that randomly assembles your 1-of-1 Surf Junkie. The script automatically picks from 100+ possible traits to generate a unique art piece out of more than one billion combinations! No one who gets a Surf Junkie NFT in the minting process (including the artist and the project founders) will know the exact Surf Junkies they will get. 21Can someone steal my Surf Junkie NFT?

In theory, they can't. The blockchain is safer than most technologies we use daily (like our online banks). Hackers can only get access to your digital wallet if you give them this access. There's a famous technique used by malicious actors called "phishing," where they find ingenious ways to make you give them your information. SO, BE SUPER CAUTIOUS. Scammers are everywhere. They pretend to be part of our official team, create a fake website, offer you a Surf Junkie for half of the market price, etc. Once you trust them, you open the doors for them to enter. But don't worry. Just be alert. When the time comes, we'll also help you to stay safe.
