01What is an NFT?

NFTs allow you to buy and sell ownership of unique digital items and keep track of who owns them using the blockchain (think of the blockchain as a giant global spreadsheet). NFT stands for “non-fungible token,” and it can technically contain anything digital, including drawings, animated GIFs, songs, or items in video games. Although anyone can copy and paste that digital item, the original NFT can always be verified and picked out among all the copies.
