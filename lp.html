03Why buy a Surf Junkie NFT when you can copy+paste it?

Buying a surf junkie NFT at the initial sale won't be like choosing a product on a shelf. Here's how it works: once you confirm your purchase, the smart contract runs a code that randomly assembles your 1-of-1 Surf Junkie. To create a single art piece, the script automatically picks from 100+ possible traits and generates a unique art piece out of more than 1 billion combinations! No one who gets a Surf Junkie NFT in the minting process (including the artist and the project founders) will know the exact Surf Junkies they will get.
