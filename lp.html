01

our vision

We are a community of surf addicts on a mission. First, We'll use NFTs to create the dopest surf club in the world. Then, We'll turn the SURF JUNKIES into a legit global brand. How? Community power. We got surf junkies in Hollywood, in Silicon Valley, on Wall Street. We got surf junkies who are engineers, designers, life guards, business owners, and rockstars. We are everywhere. And junkies are always there for other junkies. Get ready. The swell is coming.

02

THE CLUB

Imagine a club working nonstop to offer you surf-related perks and benefits. Stuff like access to wave pools, chats with pro athletes, exclusive apparel, deals on surf trips, and once-in-a-lifetime experiences.

03

THE NFTS

At the Surf Junkie Club, we'll use NFTs as our membership cards. Think of your Surf Junkie NFT as a lifetime ticket for all the fun and benefits the club will offer.

04

REAL ART

All Surf Junkie NFTs are 1-of-1 artworks created by the artist and surfer Marcello Serpa. Marcello is one of the most awarded designers in the world and co-founder of the project. Each Surf Junkie NFT unlocks access to the hi-res artwork and grants you IP over the character.

05

SCARCITY & VALUE

Out of the 35MM+ surfers in the world, the Surf Junkie Club will have room for only 0.03%. As NFTs pick up steam, more and more surfers will want to join the club, creating a high demand for our limited membership supply. Over time, beyond being a ticket for fun, your Surf Junkie NFT can become a priceless asset.

06

INITIAL PRICE

The club is for the junkiest, not the richest. We will set a fair price (TBD) for the Surf Junkie NFTs. But note that after the initial sale, the market is king and prices can go up. So, help us spread the word. Let's give all surf junkies a chance to get their NFTs at a fair price.
